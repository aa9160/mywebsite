Napoli

SSC Napoli, commonly referred to as Napoli, is an Italian professional football club based in the city of Naples that plays in Serie A 1 . The club has a long and rich history, dating back to 1904, and has been home to some of the most brilliant footballers ever, such as Diego Maradona 2 . Napoli has won numerous titles, including multiple Serie A championships and Coppa Italia trophies 2 . The team's home grounds have included Stadio Giorgio Ascarelli, Stadio Arturo Collana, and Stadio San Paolo, with the current stadium being Stadio Diego Armando Maradona 2 . Napoli is known for its strong fan base and the city's deep devotion to the team, with Diego Maradona being a central figure in the club's history 4 . Upon his passing in 2020, the Mayor of Naples declared, "Maradona è Napoli" (Maradona is Naples) 4 . The city's residents refer to Maradona as "Dios" (God) and even place him, in his sky-blue Società Sportiva Calcio (S.S.C.) Napoli jersey, among the city's centuries-old traditional handcrafted nativities 4 .Some of the most notable achievements in Napoli's history include: Winning the league title and cup in 1987, making history as the first southern Italian team to achieve such success 3 . Securing a second Serie A title in 1990 3 . Reaching the final of the UEFA Cup in 1989 2 . Overall, SSC Napoli is a highly successful football club with a rich history and a strong fan base, making it one of the most important clubs in Italian football.
